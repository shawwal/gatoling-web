# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 21st, 2023

**Welcome to Gatoling!**

At Gatoling, we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our mobile application. By using Gatoling, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

## Information Collection and Use

### Data Storage

Gatoling may save certain data, including user preferences and progress, on your device. This data is stored locally and is not shared with third parties.

### Microphone Access

Gatoling may request access to your device's microphone for pronunciation and speech recognition exercises. Access to the microphone is only used within the app and is not shared externally.

## Advertising and Analytics

### AdMob

Gatoling uses the Google AdMob platform to display advertisements within the app. AdMob may collect and use non-personal information, such as device identifiers and user location, to provide relevant ads. For more information on how AdMob handles data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

### Analytics

Gatoling may use analytics tools to track user behavior within the app. These tools help us understand how users interact with our app and improve the user experience. All data collected is anonymized and used for internal purposes only.

## Security

Gatoling takes the security of your data seriously. We implement industry-standard measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

## Changes to this Privacy Policy

Gatoling reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy as needed. Any changes will be posted here, and the effective date will be updated accordingly. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed of any modifications.
